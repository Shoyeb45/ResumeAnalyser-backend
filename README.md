## Overview

Intelli Resume is a comprehensive resume management platform that helps job seekers optimize their resumes for Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS). By analyzing your resume against specific job descriptions using AI, it provides actionable insights to improve your chances of getting noticed by recruiters.

### Why Intelli Resume?

- **AI-Powered Analysis**: Leverages OpenAI to match resumes with job descriptions
- **ATS Optimization**: Ensures your resume passes through automated screening systems
- **Professional Templates**: Build resumes using proven, recruiter-friendly formats
- **Secure & Private**: Enterprise-grade authentication with JWT and OAuth
